6.1 Studies Help

This section allows you to view all the studies that are available to you. Studies are indicated in Bold, while the rest are sites within a study. All studies are ordered through their Name.
You can choose to reorder the list of studies by toggling the ascending or descending arrow located next to the Name header. Similarly, you can also reorder the studies list using the study’s Unique Identifier, Principal Investigator, Facility Name, or Date Created, and toggle their respective ascending or descending arrows.

With the Find function, located at the top of the study list, you can search for a set of studies based on their Name, Unique Identifier, Principal Investigator, or Facility Name.

In addition to viewing all the studies, you are also presented with a link to Create a New Study.

The Administer Studies matrix displays the following columns:

1. Name This cell indicates the Name of each study.
2. Unique Identifier This cell indicates the Unique Identifier of each study.
3. OID This cell indicates the Object Identifier (OID) of each study.
4. Principal Investigator This cell indicates the Principal Investigator of each study.
5. Facility Name This cell indicates the Facility where the study is being conducted.
6. Date Created This cell indicates the date the study was created.
7. Status This cell indicates the status of each study. It could be available, removed, locked or frozen.
8. Actions (depending on role) View

Clicking on the View icon will allow you to see all the details associated with the particular study.

Edit

Clicking on the Remove icon will allow you to remove the particular study. From this point on, only the administrator can access and reverse this change if necessary.

Remove

Clicking on the Remove icon will remove the study.

Restore

Clicking on the Restore icon will allow you to restore the particular study. From this point on the study will be available, unless the study is removed.
