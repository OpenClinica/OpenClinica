3.2 Study Audit Log Help

This section allows you to view the study log for your current study. The list provides you with the Study Subject ID, Secondary Subject ID, Study Subject OID, Date of Birth, Person ID, Created By as well as Status of the Study Subject.

You can choose to reorder the list of subjects by clicking on the name header. The ascending or descending arrow located next to the name header indicates the sorting. If you click the 3rd time the sorting is disabled.

If you want to see the whole audit log for a specific Study Subject ID click on the View icon under Actions. A popup window will open and displays all actions concerning this subject.

In the top of the table you can use the green arrows to navigate through the pages. If you have less than 15 subjects in your study the arrows are greyed out, which means that there is only one page to display.
With the drop-down menu next to the arrows you can choose how many subjects should be displayed on one page (15, 25, or 50).

You can filter by entering free text or by selecting a pre-defined choice from the drop-down menu, in the grey boxes underneath each name header. To apply the filter select the Apply Filter link.
To reset the filter click on the Clear Filter link next to it.
